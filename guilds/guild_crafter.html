Guild Perks

Level 1 - Apprentice Craftsman

  • Gain access to basic crafting materials and tools at discounted rates from guild suppliers.
  • The guild will provide training in basic crafting techniques and designs.
  • Access to guild-certified workshops, allowing the creation of simple items with guild approval.

Level 2 - Journeyman

  • Ability to commission minor magical items or specialized tools from other guild members.
  • Gain exclusive access to rare crafting materials like special woods or enchanted metals.
  • The guild will promote your work in local markets, increasing your reputation and client base.

Level 3 - Master Craftsman

  • Ability to request custom magical enhancements for weapons or armor.
  • Access to a team of skilled artisans for large-scale projects, such as constructing a building or crafting complex artifacts.
  • Priority use of guild-owned resources, such as exclusive workshops and crafting spaces.

Level 4 - Guild Councillor

  • The guild will provide sponsorship for high-profile commissions, such as work for nobles or the royal family.
  • Ability to access legendary crafting materials, such as dragonbone or mithral.
  • The guild will assign apprentices to work under your command, increasing your productivity and output.

Level 5 - Grandmaster Artisan

  • Complete control over city-wide crafting regulations, dictating material supply and pricing.
  • The ability to monopolize specific markets, ensuring your products are the only ones legally sold in certain areas.
  • The guild will grant you access to their most secret techniques, allowing the creation of legendary or unique magical items.
