The Merchants' Guild

"Mastering commerce through control, wealth, and influence."

Guild Perks

Level 1 - Apprentice Merchant

  • Access to exclusive trading routes within the city.
  • Buy and sell common goods at a 10% discount within guild markets.
  • Basic legal protection from city regulations for smaller traders.

Level 2 - Trader

  • Access to guild-backed trade licenses for expansion into restricted areas.
  • Priority in city markets for stall placement and trade permits.
  • Introduction to valuable merchant contacts for partnership opportunities.

Level 3 - Master Trader

  • Secure loans or investment capital from the guild for large ventures.
  • Hire discounted mercenaries from the Order of the Silver Shield.
  • Influence market trends through guild manipulation of prices.

Level 4 - Guild Councillor

  • Use guild influence to sabotage competitors' shipments or trade routes.
  • Access to luxury and exotic goods unavailable to the public.
  • Control smaller merchants, dictating trade policies in certain areas of the city.

Level 5 - Guildmaster’s Circle

  • Free access to major trade routes and smuggling operations known to the guild.
  • Total control over key markets, with the ability to monopolize certain goods.
  • Political influence with the city’s ruling class, manipulating trade laws in favor of the guild.
